The Big Bang - reason why we were born

In the age of digital and AI revolution, we understand that every organization is going through the following 3 pain points.

  1. People costs are going higher and higher. Be it FTE (Full Time Employees) or Contractors/Vendors.
  2. The growth of no-code/low-code technologies/products/platforms are on the rise and thereby causing hard dependencies.
  3. Technological sophistication is also going up with Cloud Adoption.
  4. Organizations are being bought into more complex ways to doing things when simple techniques exist.

We realized that these problems will only grow as time proceeds and cause pain for businesses to evolve, scale and be profitable. Thus, we were born with an aim to solve these pain points.

Who are we

We bring more than 18 to 20 years of industry experience and wealth of knowledge to help our customers achieve their objectives with maximum value gain.
