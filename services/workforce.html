At {{site.title}}, we understand that every project is unique and requires a workforce that is flexible, scalable, and tailored to your specific needs. Whether you're building a new team or augmenting your existing workforce, our Workforce as a Service (WaaS) offerings provide various engagement models that allow you to quickly adapt to business demands.

Fixed Price Model

In this model, we deliver a pre-defined scope of work for a fixed price. Ideal for projects with clear requirements and deliverables.

  • Ramp-Up Time: Short to medium, depending on project complexity.
  • Cost to Customer: Fixed, agreed upon upfront.
  • Best For: Well-defined projects with specific timelines and deliverables.

When to Choose: If your project has clear requirements and the scope is unlikely to change, the Fixed Price model offers predictability in both cost and timeline.

Time & Material Model

In this model, you pay for the actual time and resources spent on the project. Ideal for evolving projects or those with changing requirements.

  • Ramp-Up Time: Quick, as resources can be allocated rapidly.
  • Cost to Customer: Variable, based on time and resources used.
  • Best For: Projects with evolving or undefined requirements.

When to Choose: If your project scope is likely to change or you prefer flexibility in resource allocation, Time & Material is the most adaptable model for continuous development and iteration.

Dedicated Team Model

We provide you with a team of skilled professionals who work exclusively on your project. Ideal for long-term projects requiring a stable team.

  • Ramp-Up Time: Medium, as team assembly and familiarization with your processes are necessary.
  • Cost to Customer: Monthly, based on team size and skillset.
  • Best For: Long-term projects requiring consistent collaboration.

When to Choose: If your project requires long-term engagement and dedicated focus, this model ensures you have a team fully committed to your success.

Outcome-Driven Model

We are compensated based on the outcomes and key deliverables we achieve. This model aligns incentives directly with business results.

  • Ramp-Up Time: Short, as the team is focused on delivering measurable outcomes.
  • Cost to Customer: Linked to performance and outcomes.
  • Best For: Projects with clear KPIs or business goals.

When to Choose: If you are looking to minimize risk and ensure that the workforce is fully aligned with your business outcomes, this model is ideal for outcome-focused projects.

Managed Services Model

We take complete responsibility for managing your IT operations or a specific business function, freeing your team to focus on core activities. This model brings in KTLO (Keeps the Lights On) support services and Sprint based enhancement capability to maintain your platform/product on a 24*7 or a 24*5 model.

  • Ramp-Up Time: Medium, as onboarding processes and knowledge transfer are key.
  • Cost to Customer: Monthly or yearly, based on the scope of services.
  • Best For: Businesses looking to outsource specific tasks or functions.

When to Choose: If you need a trusted partner to manage your operations, allowing you to focus on strategic growth, Managed Services is a comprehensive solution.

Hybrid Model

A flexible combination of different engagement models to suit complex needs. We tailor a model based on your project scope and evolving requirements.

  • Ramp-Up Time: Depends on the combination of models chosen.
  • Cost to Customer: Flexible, based on the models selected.
  • Best For: Projects requiring flexibility and custom solutions.

When to Choose: If your project has varying needs and requires a blend of different engagement models, we can create a tailored Hybrid approach to ensure optimal delivery.

At {{site.title}}, we understand the importance of flexibility. Our workforce solutions allow you to scale up rapidly when project demands increase and downsize just as quickly when needed, ensuring cost efficiency without compromising on quality.

  • Quick Ramp-Up: We can assemble teams within days to get your project moving fast.
  • Downsize with Ease: Our flexible models ensure that you can reduce team size as soon as project demands decrease.
  • Customer-Centric Approach: We prioritize your needs, adjusting our workforce to ensure you have the right talent at the right time, all while minimizing costs.

Metrics for Success

Our Workforce as a Service ensures successful project delivery through measurable outcomes. Key metrics we track include:

  • Team Ramp-Up Time: Measure how quickly we can mobilize a team based on project needs.
  • Cost Efficiency: Ensure cost savings by adjusting team sizes based on workload.
  • Performance KPIs: Track workforce performance through key indicators, ensuring optimal delivery and quality.
  • Customer Satisfaction: Regular feedback loops to ensure we meet or exceed customer expectations.
